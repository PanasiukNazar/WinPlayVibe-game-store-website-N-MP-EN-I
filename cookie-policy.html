Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how our online store for PC and console games uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your shopping experience. By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies as described here.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, improve site functionality, and provide personalized content.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable features like shopping cart management, user login, and secure checkout.

2. Performance Cookies

Performance cookies collect anonymous data about how visitors use our site, such as pages visited and errors encountered. This helps us optimize site performance and improve your experience.

3. Functionality Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences, like language selection and display settings, to provide a more personalized shopping experience.

4. Advertising Cookies

Advertising cookies track your browsing habits to show relevant game promotions and offers tailored to your interests.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Maintain your shopping cart and user session
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Customize content and promotions based on your preferences
  • Enhance website security and prevent fraudulent activity

Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of our store, such as checkout and login features.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services to set cookies on your device, such as analytics providers and advertising partners. These third parties are responsible for their own cookie policies.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Please review this page regularly for updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our use of cookies, please contact us. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe shopping experience.
